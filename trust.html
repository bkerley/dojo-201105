Trust Graph

Given a graph of how much seven people trust each other, find the most trustworthy path between all 21 possible pairs.

Trust is positive when trustworthy, negative when untrustworthy. If a link isn't specified, assume it's negative infinity. Trust is mutual/bidirectional.

Inputs

Each line is a name, a trust rating, and a name.

trust.txt

Alice 5 Bob
Bob -5 Clyde
Clyde 10 Daniella
Daniella 3 Evan
Evan 6 Francine
Francine -10 Gladys
Gladys -10 Alice
Alice 10 Daniella
Alice 10 Clyde
Clyde 5 Gladys
Gladys 9 Evan
Francine 2 Bob
Bob 5 Gladys
